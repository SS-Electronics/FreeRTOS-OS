1/*
2 * gpio_app.h — Application-level GPIO API
3 *
4 * Combines direct driver access (for fast, zero-latency operations) with
5 * management-queue operations (for delayed or ISR-deferred commands).
6 *
7 * Use the async variants from ISR or performance-critical contexts where
8 * you cannot afford to call the driver directly. Use gpio_read() for
9 * immediate pin-state sampling (bypasses the management thread).
10 *
11 * This file is part of FreeRTOS-OS Project.
12 */
