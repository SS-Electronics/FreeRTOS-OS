1/*
2 * sysmem.c — Newlib heap allocation via _sbrk (bare-metal ARM)
3 *
4 * This file is part of FreeRTOS-OS Project.
5 *
6 * _sbrk() is the low-level heap extension hook used by malloc and the C
7 * library. Grows the heap from _end (top of .bss) upward and guards against
8 * encroaching on the reserved MSP stack region defined by the linker script.
9 *
10 * Linker symbols required:
11 * _end — first address past .bss (heap start)
12 * _estack — top of RAM (stack origin)
13 * _Min_Stack_Size — minimum stack reservation
14 *
15 * These stubs are architecture-agnostic — they compile for any arm-none-eabi
16 * target whose linker script exports the symbols above.
17 */
